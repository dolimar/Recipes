chicken

This Italian dressing chicken marinade is a super simple but delicious way to add flavor before grilling.

Ingredients
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
Steps
  1. Whisk salad dressing, garlic powder, and salt together in a shallow baking dish; add chicken breasts .
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Remove chicken from marinade and shake off excess
  4. Cook chicken on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear
